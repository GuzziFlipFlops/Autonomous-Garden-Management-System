# Cost Breakdown

The project is designed around a target core platform cost around $250. This is a target, not a final claim.

## Cost-Saving Decisions

- Around 16 feet of aluminum channel from Lowe's as the main frame material
- DS3240 servos repurposed as drivetrain motors
- ESP32-P4 high-level controller instead of a Raspberry Pi 5-class SBC
- STM32 F103 Blue Pill boards for low-level control
- Low-cost ODrive-style FOC mower controller, around $40
- Commodity BTS7960 motor drivers
- Printable and hand-built mechanical parts

## Cost Risks

- Battery selection may dominate the final cost.
- Wiring, connectors, fuses, terminals, and fasteners add up quickly.
- Outdoor reliability may require stronger parts than the first prototype uses.
- Spare parts should be counted once field testing begins.
