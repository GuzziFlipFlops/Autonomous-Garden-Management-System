# Mechanical Design

The mechanical platform uses around 16 feet of aluminum channel from Lowe's as the main frame material. This keeps the chassis inexpensive, repairable, and easy to change while the design is still evolving.

## Frame

The aluminum-channel frame is intended to be cheap but robust. It also has slight compliance, which may help the robot handle rough terrain compared with a fully rigid chassis. That benefit still needs field testing.

## Layout

The robot is divided into two main sections:

- Front section: drivetrain, mower module, mower lift, battery placement, and mower electronics.
- Rear section: hose turret, base-station hose interface, and modular garden tooling.

The front section is mostly complete. The rear section is in progress.

## Modularity

The rear area is being designed around modular garden tooling. The first major module is the hose turret. A small rear-mounted pump/container module may be added later for controlled liquid application, with water-only testing first.
