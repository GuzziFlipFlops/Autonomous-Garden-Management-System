# Frame

The frame uses around 16 feet of aluminum channel from Lowe's as a low-cost structural material.

## Notes

- Cheap and easy to source
- Strong enough for early outdoor testing
- Easy to cut, make holes in, and modify
- Slightly compliant compared with a fully rigid chassis

## Status

The front section is mostly complete. The rear section is still being developed around the hose turret and modular tooling area.
