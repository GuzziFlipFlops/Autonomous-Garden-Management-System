# Lift Tests

## Static Checks

- Inspect rope path.
- Inspect knots, pulleys, and attachment points.
- Check for rubbing or sharp edges.
- Confirm mower module clears the frame through its travel.

## Motion Tests

- Raise and lower the mower without blade power.
- Measure repeatability.
- Check for rope stretch.
- Check for frame flex.
- Repeat tests under realistic mower weight.

## Safety

Do not test mower-powered operation until lift behavior is predictable.
