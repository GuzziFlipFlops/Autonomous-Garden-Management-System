# Drivetrain Tests

## Bench Checks

- Verify wiring polarity.
- Verify motor direction.
- Verify encoder direction.
- Confirm emergency stop or main disconnect works.
- Test with mower disabled.

## Low-Speed Driving

- Drive forward at low speed.
- Drive backward at low speed.
- Turn left and right.
- Check motor driver temperatures.
- Check for loose wiring or frame flex.

## Outdoor Tests

- Test on flat ground.
- Test on grass.
- Test turning on grass.
- Log current, runtime, and failures where practical.
